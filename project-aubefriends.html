AUBeFRIENDS Mobile App

University Project – Flutter, Firebase

AUBeFRIENDS is a mobile app designed to help students at the American University of Beirut connect and form meaningful friendships. The goal was to create a digital space where students could meet others with similar interests and backgrounds.

The app features an onboarding flow, profile creation with interests, and a matching system that helps students find friends based on compatibility. I collaborated with a team to develop the frontend using **Flutter**, while also contributing to the overall UI and experience of the application.

Firebase was used for backend services, including authentication, profile storage, and real-time data handling. The app was part of a university course project and helped us explore full app design, user interaction, and connection logic.

Our project was selected as one of the top apps out of more than 30 submissions to be showcased during AUB's **Demo Day** — an event where professors, deans, and industry professionals attend to discover and celebrate the most innovative student work.
