Bun Bar Website Design

Freelance Project – Web Design (Figma)

Bun Bar is a bold and vibrant restaurant concept that needed a unique online presence. I was approached as a freelance designer to create the full web layout and UI identity of the brand.

The project focused entirely on the design side — from concept sketches to full UI mockups in Figma. I created a visual identity that matched the energy and attitude of the brand: playful fonts, strong contrast, big visuals, and a fun scroll experience.

This is currently a static design project, but it lays the foundation for future development. The layout includes a bold homepage, product previews, and interactive menu sections — all designed with responsiveness and user engagement in mind.
